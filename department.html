Department of Computational Sciences

The Brainware University School of Computational and Applied Sciences or the Applied Science School offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in BCA and MCA. the fundamentals of computational science equip students with the latest know-how of mathematical techniques, parallel programming, software development and branches of research. The programme curriculum is designed by the top brains from the industry and academia. Hands-on training in multiple real-world projects through knowledge partnerships and industry-institute tie-ups keeps the students informed about the latest advancements.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Computer Applications
  • BSC Information Technology
  • Master of Computer Applications
